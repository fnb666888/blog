So you, too, come from the sky! Which is your planet?

Straight ahead of him, nobody can go very far...

Perhaps I am a little like the grown-ups. I have had to grow old.

You know—one loves the sunset, when one is so sad...

If someone loves a flower, of which just one single blossom grows in all the millions and millions of stars, it is enough to make him happy just to look at the stars.

For she did not want him to see her crying. She was such a proud flower...

From year to year the planet has turned more rapidly and the orders have not been changed!

When one wishes to play the wit, he sometimes wanders a little from the truth.

Whomever I touch, I send back to the earth from where he came.

And he lay down in the grass and cried.

The grain, which is also golden, will bring me back the thought of you.

They are in a great hurry," said the little prince. "What are they looking for?

What I see here is nothing but a shell. What is most important is invisible.

The eyes are blind. One must look with the heart.

One runs the risk of weeping a little, if one lets himself be tamed...

Here it is. Let me go on by myself.

And there is sweetness in the laughter of all the stars.

And no grown-up will ever understand that this is a matter of so much importance!
